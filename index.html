Harry Potter: Quidditch Champions invites fans to be the first to play

Publish on 30th Apr 2023

eady to fly? Harry Potter: Quidditch Champions is opening limited playtesting and you can sign up to be among the first to play.

Grab your broom and gear up!

Harry Potter: Quidditch Champions is a fast-paced, online multiplayer game featuring the world's most iconic magical sport. From Warner Bros.
